WEIGHT LOSS WEEKLY DIET PLAN

This diet plan is designed for individuals aiming for weight loss. It focuses on balanced meals that are nutrient-dense while keeping calorie intake in check. This weekly diet plan can easily be followed and repeated.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan for Weight Loss

Add these food items and habits to your lifestyle if you are following a weight loss diet plan:
1. Include plenty of fruits and vegetables.
2. Opt for lean proteins and whole grains.
3. Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
4. Limit processed foods and sugary snacks.
5. Control portion sizes and eat mindfully.

Food Items You Can Consume in Weight Loss Diet Plan

Fruits: Apples, berries, and citrus fruits are low in calories and high in fiber.
Vegetables: Leafy greens and cruciferous vegetables are nutrient-dense and filling.
Whole Grains: Brown rice, quinoa, and oats provide fiber and keep you full.
Lean Proteins: Chicken breast, turkey, fish, and legumes support muscle maintenance.
Healthy Fats: Avocado, nuts, and seeds can help with satiety.
