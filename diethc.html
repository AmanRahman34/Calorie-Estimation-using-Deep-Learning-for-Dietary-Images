HIGH CHOLESTEROL WEEKLY DIET PLAN

This diet plan is designed for individuals with high cholesterol. It focuses on foods that help lower cholesterol levels, such as those rich in fiber, healthy fats, and antioxidants. This weekly diet plan can easily be followed and repeated.

Weekly Summary

This weekly diet plan emphasizes incorporating a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats. It is structured to help lower cholesterol levels effectively.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan for High Cholesterol

Add these food items and habits to your lifestyle if you are following a high cholesterol diet plan:
1. Include more fruits and vegetables.
2. Choose whole grains instead of refined grains.
3. Opt for healthy fats like olive oil and avocado.
4. Limit saturated fats and trans fats.
5. Increase your intake of omega-3 fatty acids.

Food Items You Can Consume in High Cholesterol Diet Plan

Fruits: Berries, apples, and citrus fruits are high in fiber and antioxidants.
Vegetables: Leafy greens and cruciferous vegetables help reduce cholesterol levels.
Whole Grains: Oats, brown rice, and quinoa are excellent choices for fiber.
Healthy Fats: Nuts, seeds, and fatty fish provide heart-healthy fats.
Legumes: Beans and lentils are rich in fiber and protein.
