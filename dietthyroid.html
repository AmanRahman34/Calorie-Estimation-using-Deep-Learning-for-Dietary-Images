THYROID FRIENDLY WEEKLY DIET PLAN

This diet plan is designed for individuals with thyroid issues, particularly hypothyroidism. It focuses on foods that support thyroid health and overall well-being.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan for Thyroid

Add these food items and habits to your lifestyle if you are following a thyroid diet plan:
1. Include iodine-rich foods like seaweed and fish.
2. Incorporate selenium-rich foods such as Brazil nuts and sunflower seeds.
3. Opt for whole grains and high-fiber foods.
4. Avoid excessive consumption of goitrogenic foods (like soy) and highly processed foods.
5. Stay hydrated and include plenty of fruits and vegetables.

Food Items You Can Consume in Thyroid Diet Plan

Fruits: Berries, bananas, and apples are good choices.
Vegetables: Leafy greens, broccoli, and carrots provide essential nutrients.
Whole Grains: Quinoa, brown rice, and oats help maintain energy levels.
Lean Proteins: Fatty fish, chicken, and legumes support muscle health.
Healthy Fats: Olive oil, avocados, and nuts are great for overall health.
