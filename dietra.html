RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS WEEKLY DIET PLAN

This diet plan is designed for individuals with rheumatoid arthritis. It focuses on foods that may help reduce inflammation and improve overall joint health.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan for Rheumatoid Arthritis

Add these food items and habits to your lifestyle if you are following a rheumatoid arthritis diet plan:
1. Include plenty of omega-3 fatty acids from fish and flaxseeds.
2. Opt for colorful fruits and vegetables rich in antioxidants.
3. Choose whole grains over refined grains.
4. Limit processed foods, added sugars, and saturated fats.
5. Stay hydrated and consider green tea for its anti-inflammatory properties.

Food Items You Can Consume in Rheumatoid Arthritis Diet Plan

Fruits: Berries, cherries, and oranges are high in antioxidants.
Vegetables: Leafy greens, broccoli, and bell peppers provide essential vitamins.
Whole Grains: Brown rice, quinoa, and oats help maintain energy levels.
Lean Proteins: Fatty fish like salmon and plant-based proteins like beans and lentils support muscle health.
Healthy Fats: Olive oil, avocados, and nuts promote heart health and reduce inflammation.
