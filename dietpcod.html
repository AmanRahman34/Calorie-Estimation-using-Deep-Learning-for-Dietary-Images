PCOD FRIENDLY WEEKLY DIET PLAN

This diet plan is designed for individuals with PCOD, focusing on foods that support hormonal balance and overall health.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan for PCOD

Add these food items and habits to your lifestyle if you are following a PCOD diet plan:
1. Include high-fiber foods like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
2. Opt for lean proteins, such as chicken and legumes.
3. Incorporate healthy fats from sources like avocados and nuts.
4. Avoid refined sugars and processed foods.
5. Stay hydrated and maintain regular exercise.

Food Items You Can Consume in PCOD Diet Plan

Fruits: Berries, apples, and oranges are great choices.
Vegetables: Leafy greens, broccoli, and carrots provide essential nutrients.
Whole Grains: Quinoa, brown rice, and oats help maintain energy levels.
Lean Proteins: Eggs, chicken, fish, and legumes support muscle health.
Healthy Fats: Olive oil, nuts, and seeds are good for overall health.
