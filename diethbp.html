HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE WEEKLY DIET PLAN

Weekly Summary

This diet plan is designed to help manage high blood pressure effectively. It includes a variety of healthy food items that are low in sodium and rich in nutrients. Follow this plan to maintain a balanced diet while keeping your blood pressure in check.

Do's And Don'ts While Following Diet Plan

Add these food items and habits in your lifestyle if you are following high blood pressure diet plan:

  • Eat less salt
  • Include more fruits and vegetables
  • Include whole grain cereals
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Include lean meat, fish, and poultry
  • Include low-fat dairy products
Try to avoid these habits if you are following high blood pressure diet plan:
  • Alcohol
  • Smoking
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Sleep deprivation

Food Items You Can Easily Consume In High Blood Pressure Diet Plan

Banana: Portable, low in sodium, and rich in potassium.
Spinach: Low in calories and high in potassium, folate, and magnesium.
Celery: Contains phthalides that relax artery walls.
Oatmeal: High in fiber and helps maintain healthy blood pressure.
Avocado: Reduces blood pressure and cholesterol levels.
Watermelon: Contains L-citrulline, known to lower blood pressure.
Beetroot: Rich in nitrates, improves blood flow.
Oranges: High in vitamin C, supports blood pressure health.
